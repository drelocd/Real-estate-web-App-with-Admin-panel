Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make an application on our website.

Effective Date: 1st June 2023

At RealMark Properties, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and manage the personal information obtained from individuals applying for jobs through our platform. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect:

We collect personal information from individuals applying for job opportunities through our platform. This information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Full name
  • Contact information (email address, phone number, mailing address)
  • Resume or curriculum vitae
  • Employment history
  • Educational background
  • Any other information provided in the job application

2. Use of Information:

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process and assess job applications
  • To communicate with applicants regarding their applications
  • To evaluate qualifications and suitability for job positions
  • To maintain accurate records of job applications
  • To improve our recruitment processes

3. Job Application Fee:

RealMark charges a refundable fee for processing job applications.

This fee is intended to cover administrative costs associated with the application process and is refundable*

4. Information Security:

We take appropriate measures to safeguard the personal information we collect. This includes secure storage, restricted access, and protection against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Disclosure:

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer personal information to outside parties unless we provide advance notice to the applicant.

This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy:

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact Information:

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@realmark.co.ke.

By submitting a job application and paying the application fee, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
